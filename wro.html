About the Event

The World Robot Olympiad (WRO) is a global robotics competition that brings together young people from all over the world to develop their creativity, design, and problem-solving skills through challenging and educational robotics competitions.

Future Innovators

Our team participated in the Future Innovators category, developing a prototype capable of measuring weather conditions and alert of possible danger wirelessly. Obtained 2nd Highest American score.

Meet Our Team

We are a group of passionate individuals who love robotics and innovation. Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible and making a positive impact through technology.
